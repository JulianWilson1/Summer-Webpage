Jordans

Jordan 11's

The jordan 11 is my favorite pair of jordans. My favorite jordan 11 is the Win Like 82, which was released on November 11th, 2017. The price of the shoe right now is $225 new and $120 used. The first 11 was released in 1995. It was designed by Tinker Hatfield. The 11 retro came out five years later.

Jordan 4's

The jordan 4 is my second favorite pair of jordans. My favorite pair of 4's is the Cactus Jack by Travis Scott. The Cactus Jack released on June 9th, 2018. The current price for the Cactus Jack is $330 new and $295 used. The 4's were released in 1989, then the retro's came out in 1999. The jordan 4's were designed by Tinker Hatfield.

Jordan 12's

The jordan 12 is my third favorite pair of jordans. My favorite pair of 12's is the OVO's by Drake. The price for the OVO 12 is $635 new and $350 used. The OVO 12's released October 1st, 2016. The 12's were released in 1996 and were retroed in in 2003. The 12's were designed by Tinker Hatfield.
